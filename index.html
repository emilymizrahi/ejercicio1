Anna Wintour

Anna in Vogue

Anna Wintour has been editor-in-chief of Vogue since 1988 and artistic director at Condé Nast. She's also known for her involvement with the Metropolitain Museum of Art due to the annual fundraiser she hosts anually.

Anna's Life

Anna Wintour was born on November 3, 1949, in London, England. She was raised by one of the wealthiest family's in the UK. Her father was Charles Wintour, the editor of the London Evening Standard newspaper. Since then she has been passionate for the editorial world.

Anna's Lifestyle

The key to success is having organizational skills. Anna has a very strategic lifestyle. She tends to be very organized with her daily schedule, this way she can get done as much and in the best way posible. One of her top tips is to be yourself and be the best version of it.
